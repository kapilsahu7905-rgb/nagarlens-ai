# Two-minute demo script

## 0:00–0:20 — Problem

“Local civic complaints are fragmented. Citizens often do not know the right category or department, duplicate reports waste effort, and issues may be marked resolved without transparent evidence.”

## 0:20–1:15 — Live workflow

1. Upload a clear pothole or water-leak image.
2. Capture location and add one sentence of context.
3. Show Gemini validating the evidence, setting category, severity, urgency, safety risks and likely department.
4. Show the generated complaint and action plan.
5. Publish the issue and open it on the community board.
6. Confirm it from another incognito browser.
7. Generate an AI follow-up.

## 1:15–1:45 — Resolution intelligence

Upload an after-repair image. Show Gemini comparing before and after, explaining whether the issue is resolved, partly resolved, unresolved or unclear, and updating the timeline.

## 1:45–2:00 — Close

“NagarLens AI turns one citizen photo into a transparent path from evidence to community verification and resolution. It does not pretend to replace authorities; it helps communities produce better reports and hold progress to visible evidence.”
